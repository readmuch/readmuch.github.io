## 12. Strikethrough, Bold, and Italics

Use * or _ for Italics.

Use ** or __ for Bold.

For strikethrough, use ~~.  
For underlines, use <U></U> (uppercase U).

*Italics*

_Italics_

**Bold**

__Bold__

~~Strikethrough~~

<U>Underline</U>
